Milford Sound Tour

A private full day to the eighth wonder of the world, with a photographer beside you.

The experience

The eighth wonder, captured as you go

Carved by glaciers over millions of years, Milford Sound rises in sheer peaks from dark, still water, with waterfalls falling hundreds of metres and seals and dolphins moving through the fiord. Its beauty has drawn travellers and film-makers alike, from The Lord of the Rings to the far edges of the imagination.

This is a full day through Fiordland in your own private vehicle, with your photographer-host capturing the journey naturally. Every turn is a photograph waiting to happen, and yours to keep.

(A full day, around 12 to 13 hours.)

The details

What's included

Duration
12 to 13 hours

Your day is private from start to finish. You're collected from your Queenstown accommodation in a premium vehicle and travel south through Fiordland with curated stops along the way, a coffee break by Lake Te Anau, quiet alpine lakes, and the dramatic cliffs and waterfalls of the national park. At Milford you board a boutique small-group cruise past Mitre Peak and Stirling Falls. Throughout the day your photographer-host captures your story, and a few days later a collection of professionally edited images arrives, candid, scenic and personal, yours to keep.

The day

A typical itinerary

A private pickup from your Queenstown accommodation in a premium vehicle. The day begins beside the calm waters of Lake Wakatipu as you head south in comfort.

A pause in Te Anau for coffee and a light refreshment before you continue deeper into the heart of Fiordland.

Into the dramatic landscapes of Fiordland National Park, with stops at handpicked scenic spots for guided storytelling and professional photography.

Arrive at Milford Sound, where towering cliffs and misty peaks open up before you, the eighth wonder of the world.

Board a premium small-group cruise and glide through the fiord, past Mitre Peak, Stirling Falls and native wildlife, with tea or coffee onboard.

Begin the journey back to Queenstown, pausing for photo stops as the landscape softens in the afternoon light.

Arrive back at your accommodation by evening, with a day of memories and a collection of beautifully edited images to follow.
