Into the Southern Alps

Mount Cook Tour

A private full day among New Zealand's highest peaks and glacial lakes, with a photographer beside you.

The experience

New Zealand's highest peak, up close

Aoraki / Mount Cook, New Zealand's highest peak, rises above turquoise glacial lakes and sweeping alpine valleys within the UNESCO-listed national park. This is the heart of the Southern Alps, home to ancient glaciers and some of the clearest night skies on Earth, a landscape seen in films like The Lord of the Rings.

This is a full day into that alpine world in your own private vehicle, combining grand scenery, a little history, and photography at every stop.

The details

What's included

Duration
A full day

Your day is private from start to finish. You travel from Queenstown through the dramatic Lindis Pass to the turquoise shores of Lake Pukaki, with Aoraki / Mount Cook rising in the distance. In the national park there is the option to walk a scenic track such as the Hooker Valley or the Tasman Glacier View, paced to suit you. Throughout the day your photographer-guide captures the landscape and your place within it, and a few days later a collection of professionally edited images arrives, yours to keep.

The day

A typical itinerary

A personal pickup from your Queenstown accommodation. Settle into a premium vehicle for a day of scenic wonder and curated storytelling.

Travel through the dramatic Lindis Pass and its sweeping tussock landscapes, pausing at key viewpoints timed for soft light and minimal crowds.

Arrive at the stunning Lake Pukaki, where glacial waters reflect Aoraki / Mount Cook in the distance. Your guide captures portraits and panoramas along the turquoise shore.

Explore the alpine beauty of Mount Cook National Park. Depending on your preference and fitness, enjoy a walk such as the Hooker Valley Track, with its glaciers, swing bridges and alpine rivers, or the shorter Tasman Glacier View, with candid moments captured throughout.

Dine at the Hermitage Hotel or enjoy a relaxed picnic with a view, tailored to your preferences.

Continue to hidden gems and further stops chosen for the light and conditions, alpine meadows, braided rivers and mirror lakes, with photographs taken throughout.

Relax on the journey back to Queenstown, with comfort breaks and photo stops as time and light allow.

Arrive back at your accommodation in the evening. Within days, a curated gallery of professionally edited images follows, a visual story of your day at Aoraki / Mount Cook.
