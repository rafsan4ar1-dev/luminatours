Good to know

Frequently asked

Are Lumina tours private?+

Yes. Every Lumina Tours experience is fully private, just you and your guide. No other guests, no fixed timetable, and the pace of the day is entirely yours.

Do I receive the photographs?+

Yes. Your guide is a professional photographer who captures your experience throughout the day, and a set of professionally edited images is delivered to you after your tour.

Where do you operate?+

We're based in Queenstown and run private tours across the South Island, including Milford Sound, Aoraki / Mount Cook, Glenorchy & Paradise, Cromwell & Wanaka and Skippers Canyon.

When is the best time for stargazing?+

Stargazing and astrophotography tours run from mid-April to mid-September, on clear, moonless nights. The Milky Way is at its most spectacular in June and July, with May and August also excellent.

Can you tailor a custom itinerary?+

Absolutely. We build bespoke itineraries around your interests and can arrange add-ons such as helicopter flights and glacier landings. Just tell us what you're dreaming of.
